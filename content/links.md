## Lean

- [Real Analysis, The Game](https://adam.math.hhu.de/#/g/AlexKontorovich/RealAnalysisGame) - Great Lean project for learning the basics of real analysis
- [Natural Number Game](https://adam.math.hhu.de/#/g/leanprover-community/nng4) - The best introduction for Lean, giving a good overview of basic syntax and some of the language works
- [Lean Community](https://leanprover-community.github.io) - Lean proof assistant resources
- [Lean Game Server](https://adam.math.hhu.de/#/) - Some popular Lean games

## Russian

- [Amazing Russian](https://www.youtube.com/@AmazingRussian) - Channel with great videos for beginners and intermediate speakers, better explanation of grammar than most other channels
- [Russian Lessons . net](https://www.russianlessons.net/)
- [Russian for free](https://www.russianforfree.com/)


## French
